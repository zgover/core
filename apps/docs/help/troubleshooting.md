# Troubleshooting

## My changes aren't live

Publishing a screen creates a version that goes live — edits in the Besigner
aren't public until you publish. See
[Publish your first screen](/getting-started/publish-your-first-screen) and
[Versions & scheduled publishing](/building-sites/screens-and-layouts/overview#versions--scheduled-publishing).

## My custom domain won't verify

DNS changes can take time to propagate, and a wrong record is the usual cause.
Walk through [Troubleshoot verification](/building-sites/custom-domains/troubleshooting),
and double-check the record against [Connect a domain](/building-sites/custom-domains/connect-a-domain).

## I can't sign in

Aglyn uses Google sign-in, one session across all your workspaces, and automatic
sign-out after inactivity. See
[Signing in & sessions](/workspace-and-billing/signing-in-and-sessions). If your
email isn't verified, some actions are blocked until you verify it.

## Search isn't finding a page

Site search matches published content — screens, blog entries, and dataset
records surfaced through a repeatable screen. Make sure the content is published
and see [Site search](/building-sites/site-search/overview).

## A form submission didn't arrive

Check the inbox and your plan's monthly submission cap. See
[Forms & lead capture](/content-and-data/forms/overview).

Still stuck? [Contact support](contact.md).
