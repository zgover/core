# Frequently asked questions

## Is Aglyn really no-code?

Yes. You design pages visually in the [Besigner](/building-sites/besigner/overview),
bind them to your data, and publish — no code required. Developers can go further
with [plugins](/developers/plugins/overview) and the [REST API](/api).

## What can I build for free?

Core site building is **free**. Advanced features — commerce, workflows,
webhooks, the REST API, and more — unlock on paid plans. Each docs page notes
what it needs; see [Billing & plans](/workspace-and-billing/billing-and-plans/overview).

## How do I use my own domain?

Connect it with the self-service wizard — see
[Custom domains](/building-sites/custom-domains/overview). If verification is
stuck, check [Troubleshoot verification](/building-sites/custom-domains/troubleshooting).

## Can visitors have accounts on my site?

Yes — let visitors sign up, gate screens to members, and manage them from the
console. See [Member accounts](/guides/member-accounts).

## Can I access my data programmatically?

On the Business plan, yes — the [REST API](/api) exposes datasets and records,
contacts, sites, and form submissions, plus [webhooks](/api/integrations/webhooks).

## How is my site kept fast and secure?

Published sites are server-rendered, SEO-ready, and served over a CDN. See
[SEO](/building-sites/seo/overview) and
[Site protection](/building-sites/site-protection/overview).
