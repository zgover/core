# Invite teammates

Bring collaborators into a host so they can build and manage alongside you.

:::info Plan availability
**Paid**. Team seats are metered per tier; buy seat add-ons to grow. See
[Billing & plans](../billing-and-plans/overview.md).
:::

## Invite someone

1. Open the host's **user manager**.
2. Invite the person by email and assign a [role](custom-roles.md).
3. They accept and gain access according to that role.

## How team members act

Team members act **in the owner's tenant**, not their own — so their changes apply to your
host, and permissions are enforced across the console's APIs and surfaces. Seat limits are
enforced per tier; if you're at your limit, add a **seat add-on** before inviting more.

## Tips

- Start people on a least-privilege [role](custom-roles.md) and widen it with per-member
  overrides only where needed.
- Removing a member frees their seat.
